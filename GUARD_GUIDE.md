# Marketnera Entry - Guard Guide

Welcome to the Marketnera Entry System. As a gate guard, your role is to scan student QR codes quickly and accurately. 
This guide explains how to use the web-based scanner on your smartphone.

## 1. Getting Started

**No App Installation Required**
1. Open your mobile browser (Safari on iPhone, Chrome on Android).
2. Go to the event entry URL provided by the organizer (e.g., `https://your-event-domain.com/login`).
3. Enter your assigned email and password to log in.
4. You will be redirected automatically to the **Scanner Interface**.

## 2. Setting Up the Scanner

When you first load the scanner page:
1. **Camera Permission:** Your browser will ask for permission to use the camera. Tap **Allow**.
2. **Gate Selection:** Tap the gate selector at the top (e.g., "Gate A") to ensure your scans are logged to the correct entrance.
3. **Camera Check:** You should see a live camera feed inside the green frame.

> **Tip for Dark Environments:** If it's too dark to read QRs, look for the 🔦 **Torch** button to turn on your phone's flashlight.

## 3. Scanning QRs

Hold the student's phone so their QR code is visible inside the green scanning box. The scanner will automatically detect and read the QR.

### ✅ Entry Granted (Green Screen)
- **What it means:** The QR is valid and this is the first time it is being used.
- **What to do:** Allow the student to enter. You will see their Name and ID on the screen. The screen will stay green for a few seconds before returning to the camera automatically.

### ❌ Denied / Already Scanned (Red Screen)
- **What it means:** The QR code has *already been used* by someone else, or is invalid.
- **What to do:** Do **not** allow entry. The red screen will show when the QR was originally used and at which gate. Ask the student to visit the support desk.

## 4. Master QR Codes (VIPs)
If an organizer shows you a special **Master QR Code**, scanning it will display a **MASTER SCANNED** message. Master QRs can be scanned an unlimited number of times and are used for tracking total headcounts or VIP re-entry. Treat a Master Scan the same as a green "Entry Granted" screen.

## Troubleshooting

- **Camera not loading?** Refresh the page and make sure you tapped "Allow" for camera permissions. If you accidentally denied it, you may need to clear your browser settings.
- **Scanner frozen?** Tap the ⟳ **Reset** button at the bottom of the screen to restart the camera feed.
- **Network error?** If you see a red toast notification saying "Network Error", check your phone's internet connection. The system requires an active connection to validate tickets.
